I'm fighting now, with my beliefs.

“A creative man is motivated by the desire to achieve, not by the desire to beat others.” – Ayn Rand

There is no shortcut on the way to study.

When my life asks me:”Do you still have a choice?” I only want to answer:”Of course! I have so many choices!”

The chance is not given by your life, but by fighting for yourself.

When reality is not as good as ideal, a fighter will accept it; When reality is close to ideal, a fighter will explore their distance.

The achievements of genius is produced by 99% of sweat plus 1% of talent, let alone me.

心存敬畏，不忘初心。器识其先，文艺其从。立德立言，无问西东。
